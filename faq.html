F.A.Q.

What is a pocket router Wifi4you?

This is package which contains a modem and a router , the size is a little bigger than a business card. It guarantees you a stable, safe and fast connection up to 5 devices to the Internet without buying a local SIM card.

How many devices can I connect at one time?

You can connect up to 5 devices to 1 Wifi4you router.

How long does the battery last?

A fully charged battery works about 5-7 hours.

Where can I rent a router?

The router can be rented at airports in Moscow (Domodedovo, Sheremetyevo, Vnukovo), and in popular hotels like Cosmos, Radisson Slavyanskaya. We are actively working to expand our sales outlets. Also we will be happy to provide you with routers’ delivery service .

Can I order a router delivery to my hotel in Moscow?

Yes, you can. Please fill in the application in1 day on our site and choose the delivery term . Delivery for you will be free if you plan to rent more than 7 days .

Which documents will be required when renting Wifi4you?

We will need your passport. And also need your credit / debit card. We will sign a contact with you .

How and where can I pay?

You can pay online in our website. And also in any sale point or through the courier. We would like to inform you that in addition to rent the Internet, we will block the cost of the router in your card. After the return of the router, the amount is automatically unlocked.

Is there a cash payment?

No, our ideology is the development of Internet technologies in Russia, so we are only set up for a non-cash payment form . Perhaps in the future we will provide you with the opportunity to pay in crypto-currencies.

Is there a deposit for renting?

Yes, as mentioned above, we charge an advance payment for using the Internet, and also block the cost of the router in your card. Suppose you will fly to Russia for 10 days. Make an advance payment for 10 days of using the router. If your will change your plan, please call our general number +7(915)440-73-99 and inform the operator about the return date . The surcharge will be charged upon the return of the router.

How is the deposit refunded?

Our company will refund your deposit when you return us the router . The amount of the deposit varies and it depends on the type of the router, usually it does not exceed 5000 rubles.

Can I cancel my order?

Yes, of course you can. You need to call our operator and tell us your decision. If you have already made an online prepayment - then we will refund your money within 5 working days.
