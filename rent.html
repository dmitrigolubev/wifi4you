How to rent ?

The first step

Select the device on the site, specify the rental date and make a payment. The booking confirmation will be sent to you by e-mail.

The second step

Take your wifi4you at one of the sale places listed on this site. The exact address of issue and return will be indicated in the booking confirmation. Our courier can come to bring Wifi4you router to your place.

The third step

Return your Wifi4you at specified. Your deposit will be returned to your credit / debit card. Our courier can come to pick up the router when your order is longer than 10 days. The guide for receiving and returning the router will be sent to your e-mail after booking. Please show our staff your reservation number.

More detailed terms of rent and use please click F.A.Q.
